Staples in My Fridge

Quick Picked Onions
I use this recipe often because it is easy and they are delicious! Throw them in mac n'cheese, on top of tacos, or even on fried eggs to perk up a dish.
Miso paste
Miso is a must! I mix it with a bit of butter, or soy sauce and ginger and garlic. Mix it in with vegetables of any type, or throw it over pasta!
Muhammara
PCC calls this Syrian Pomegranate spread. I keep this because I like to eat it with tortilla chips and it is one of my favorite dips. If I am on low on time, or can't squeeze in a lunch, this makes for a yummy quick snack.
Apples and Peanut butter
Ya know, I had a thing against apples. Never ate them for quite some time. Apples and peanut butter once again serve as an awesome quick snack and fill me up when I am low on time.
